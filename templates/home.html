Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a car? +

Create an account on our website, choose the car you want, and click the Book Now button. Enter your phone number and the amount you'd like to pay. Confirm your payment, and you will receive a confirmation notification on your phone via M-PESA. It’s quick, easy, and secure!

What documents do I need? +

We require a valid driver’s license, national ID or passport, and proof of payment at pickup.

Can I cancel my booking? +

Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before your pickup time for a full refund.

Our Terms & Conditions

By using our car booking service, you agree to comply with our terms and conditions. Bookings must be made using valid personal information. All payments should be completed through the approved M-PESA system on our platform. Once a booking is confirmed, the vehicle will be reserved for the agreed duration.

Cancellations made at least 24 hours before the pickup time are eligible for a full refund. Late cancellations may attract a service charge. It is the responsibility of the renter to inspect the vehicle at the time of pickup and report any pre-existing damages.
